Biography

I’m a Ph.D. Student at the Deptartment of Computer Science and Engineering of Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur under the supervision of Dr. Gaurav Sharma and Prof. Manindra Agarwal. My area of interest comes under the broad area of Computer Vision and Machine Learning. I am supported by Visvesvaraya PhD Scheme offered by the Department of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India.

Prior to this, I have completed my Master’s Degree in Medical Imaging and Informatics from Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur. In my Master’s, I have worked in the area of Depth estimation from images and have completed my thesis under the joint supervision of Dr. Rajiv Ranjan Sahay and Prof. Pranab Kumar Dutta.
